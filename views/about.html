About Me

My name is Stephanie Rose Calma. I am currently a student at Seneca College taking up Post Graduate Certificate in Business Information Technology.

I am a cat person and I have a pet cat named "Timmy". Timmy is such a good and lovable cat. His favorite food is the brand Aozi in Salmon, Chicken and Beef flavor. He is also picky with his water, and he only drinks distilled water.

I am a great believer in the power and wisdom of the golden rule: "Do unto others as you would have them do unto you." This principle has resonated with individuals and cultures across the globe for centuries, reminding us of the importance of empathy, compassion, and treating others with utmost respect and kindness.
For me, it is important to remember that the golden rule does not imply that everyone wants to be treated in the exact same way. Rather, it encourages us to consider the unique needs and preferences of each individual. Recognizing and respecting these differences is crucial in effectively applying the golden rule.
